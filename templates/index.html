Maternal Health Risk Predictor

Analyze and predict maternal health risk levels during pregnancy using IoT-based monitoring data.

This application leverages data collected from hospitals, community clinics, and maternal healthcare centers to evaluate maternal health conditions during pregnancy. The dataset includes essential indicators such as blood pressure, blood glucose levels, heart rate, and age to predict risk intensity levels. By using machine learning, healthcare professionals and patients can identify early warning signs, enabling timely medical interventions and improved maternal care.
